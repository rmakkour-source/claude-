## Routing Logic

When the user invokes `/market <command>`, route to the appropriate sub-agent or skill.
Always read `market/business_context.md` before executing any command to remind yourself of BingeBear's pricing and target audience.

### Full Marketing Audit (`/market audit`)
This is the flagship command. It launches **5 sub-agents in parallel** to analyze BingeBear's entire marketing ecosystem simultaneously:

1. **agent market-content** → Evaluates copy, value propositions, and pain agitation (Sky TV bills).
2. **agent market-conversion** → Fixes the massive WhatsApp setup bottleneck and trial drop-offs.
3. **agent market-competitive** → Positions BingeBear against Sky TV and cheap "dodgy boxes".
4. **agent market-technical** → Audits Facebook Pixel tracking, mobile UX for older men, and site speed.
5. **agent market-strategy** → Evaluates the pricing strategy and €250 Lifetime upsell mechanics.

**BingeBear Scoring Methodology (0-100 Marketing Score):**
| Category | Weight | What it measures for BingeBear |
|----------|--------|--------------------------------|
| Setup Friction & CRO | 30% | How easy it is for a 55-year-old man to set up his Firestick. |
| Trust & Risk Reversal | 25% | Prominence of the 90-Day Guarantee and 4.8 Trustpilot score. |
| Copy & Messaging | 20% | Absence of tech jargon; effectiveness of the "Sky TV Anchor" pitch. |
| Lifetime Upsell Strategy | 15% | How aggressively and effectively the €250 plan is sold. |
| Tech & FB Tracking | 10% | Pixel health and mobile readability. |

**Composite Marketing Score** = Weighted average of the 5 categories.

### Quick Overview (`/market quick`)
A rapid 60-second assessment. DO NOT launch sub-agents. Instead:
1. Quickly review BingeBear's current bottlenecks.
2. Evaluate: Headline clarity, Free Trial CTA strength, 90-Day Guarantee visibility.
3. Output a fast dashboard with 3 strengths and 3 immediate, high-ROI fixes.
4. Limit output to 30 lines.

## BingeBear Output Standards

ALL outputs generated by this suite MUST respect these rules:
1. **Actionable over Theoretical:** Never suggest "building brand awareness". Give exact Facebook ad copy or WhatsApp scripts that can be copy-pasted today.
2. **Prioritized by Cash:** Always rank recommendations by how fast they will generate revenue (e.g., fixing the Setup Bottleneck is always Priority #1).
3. **The Lifetime Anchor:** Every strategy must ultimately lead the customer to buying the €250 Lifetime Plan (or €150/€100 split pay).
4. **No AI Buzzwords:** Never use words like "Unlock", "Delve", "Supercharge", or "Seamless". Use 6th-grade, conversational UK/IE English.
5. **File Outputs:** Always save detailed outputs to a Markdown file in the current directory, with a descriptive name, date, and executive summary.
